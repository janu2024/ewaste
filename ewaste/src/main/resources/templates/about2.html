Our Story

At e-Waste, we provide a full suite of asset management solutions.Electronics asset retirement can be a complex challenge, sometimes requiring multiple vendors to handle shipping, packaging, storage, inventory, data destruction, logistics, disposal and recycling.

This website will allow user to sell his/her electronic waste to e-waste companies and help the environment free from E-Waste. In addition we are going to sell online green products which we have made out of e-waste.

The average life span of an electronic device is in the range of 5 to 7 years. After its unavailability, they are just thrown away in garbage or to the recycle. Most of the electronic waste is not recycled. Recycling of most of the electronic waste is possible in today’s world. There are many e-waste companies which are recycling e-waste. This website will provide platform between common people and e-waste companies.

Our Mission

We are making a general application which can be used according to the customer needs. Our application will work on the concept of Web Application. Through our application users will be able to find the nearest e-waste collection points and schedule e-waste pickup from them doorstep. With options like Reuse, Recycle, Resell users will have the flexibility of deciding what they want to do.

The main aim of the concept is we can use it according to the client needs. It will also reduce the pollution which is increased by the “Electronic Waste” of Home, Office, and Company Machines. For example Remote, Microwave, Laptops, Hard disk, Microprocessors, Circuit Board, Wires.
